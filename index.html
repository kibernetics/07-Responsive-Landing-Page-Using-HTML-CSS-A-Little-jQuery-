How it works

Our network can read and understand text of over 10000 Twitter accounts. Among them are the largest corporations, influential people, developers, national governments, financial institutions and exchanges. Algorithm checks reaction of the price cryptocurrency, which was discussed in the read tweets. If a price impulse response is detected, you receive an instant message in the telegram channel. This helps traders to see the beginning of a local trend in order to make profitable trades according to their trading strategies. Algorithm has protection against false price movements, we are constantly working to improve the quality of signals.
